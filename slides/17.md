### How to start

```js
├── docs
│   ├── README.md (Source of the content)
│   └── .vuepress (Optional)
│       ├── components (Optional)
│       ├── theme (Optional)
│       │   └── Layout.vue
│       ├── styles (Optional)
│       │   ├── index.styl
│       │   └── palette.styl
│       ├── config.js (Optional, config center, could be made also in yaml)
│       └── enhanceApp.js (Optional)
└── package.json (Manage dependencies)
```
